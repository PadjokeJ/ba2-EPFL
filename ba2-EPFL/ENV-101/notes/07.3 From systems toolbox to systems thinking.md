**Leverage points** : ways to intervene within a system (ordered from least to most impactful)
- Technical leverage points
	- Looks at the stocks/flows and other numbers
- Feedback leverage points
	- Target feedback loops, adjusting their strength
- Social leverage points
	- Social relations between the human population and the system - could be having more information, or empowering people
- Transcendental leverage points
	- Mindset or paradigm which is the source of the system

Systems traps can occur:
- Having too little knowledge of the relations within the systems
- Rather from structure of the system than from individuals

> [!Example]
> **Policy resistance**  
> Trap: parts of the system try to pull the system towards their goals

> [!Example]
> **Tragedy of the commons**  
> Depleting/degradation a natural (common) resource for a gain
