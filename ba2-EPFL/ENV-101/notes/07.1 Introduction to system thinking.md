Week 4's exercise -> systems thinking
- Connections of species in an ecosystem

> [!Definition]
> A ==system== is a set of *interconnected* elements that are *coherently organized* in a way **that achieves something**

Three parts of the definition
- Parts (set of elements)
- Relationships (interconnections)
- Purpose (what it achieves)

# Systems models

**Stocks** - quantities that can accumulate over time

**Flows** - changes in the quantities of stocks

**Source/sinks** - stocks flow from sources and/or to sinks

![[Pasted image 20260326134512.png]]

> [!Example] Example -- bathtub
> - Source: faucet
> - Stock: water in the tub
> - Sink: the drain of the tub

We are mostly interested in modelling stocks
