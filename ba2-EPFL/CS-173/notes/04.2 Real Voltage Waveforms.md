Binary values are actually represented as voltage levels
- 0 : low
- 1 : high

We also have thresholds for the circuit to register a binary state
- $V_{0, \text{max}}$ the maximum bound for *low*
- $V_{1, \text{min}}$ the minimum bound *high*
- In between, they are undefined

In reality, circuits take time to transition from a state to another
- $t_{r}$ - the rise time 
- $t_{f}$ - the fall time

It also takes time for a gate to register that something is happening -> we call that *propagation delay* $t_{p}$
